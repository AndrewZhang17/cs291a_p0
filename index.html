Andrew Zhang's Definitive Melon Ratings

I have compiled data from my personal experiences to rate a few melons based on taste, and appearance. These ratings (which are infallible) are on a scale from 1 to 10, with 10 being the best. Here is a list of the melons I am rating:

  • Bitter Melon
  • Cantaloupe
  • Honeydew
  • Watermelon
Melon Image Taste Appearance Notes
Bitter Melon 1 6 Tastes horrible. A bit ugly but has cool texture.
Cantaloupe 7 8 Nice color. Cool skin texture. Nice, sweet flavor.
Honeydew 5 4 Tastes like slightly sweet water. Looks boring. Bojack does not like.
Watermelon 10 10 Exquisite flavor. Picturesque appearance. Best melon. In fact, best fruit.
These are the definitive ratings for a few melons. If you disagree you are wrong. Go watermelons.
